# Hale — Support

Hale is a private, local-first health & fitness app — food, workouts, and body
composition, all stored on your device. Thanks for using it!

## Get help or report a problem

Hale is built by a solo developer, and the fastest ways to reach me are:

- **In the app:** Settings → **Send feedback** — file a bug or a feature idea. It opens
  a pre-filled email with a few basic diagnostics so I can help faster.
- **On TestFlight:** take a screenshot anywhere in the app and send it with a note through
  TestFlight's built-in feedback.
- **Email:** **zander.halverson99@gmail.com**

I read every message and aim to reply within a few days.

## Common questions

**Where is my data stored?**
Everything you log lives in a local database on your device. There's no account and no
server — see the [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/officialxndr/KUfit/blob/main/docs/PRIVACY.md).

**How do I back up or move my data?**
Settings → **Data & backup** → **Export data** creates a backup file you can save or move
to another device, and **Import data** restores it.

**How do I delete my data?**
Settings → **Data & backup** → **Wipe all data**, or simply delete the app — that removes
the on-device database entirely.

**Are the body-fat and calorie numbers exact?**
They're estimates to guide you (from your weigh-ins, a DEXA baseline, or the U.S. Navy tape
method), not medical advice.

**Do I need to create an account?**
No. Hale has no accounts, no ads, and no tracking.

## Privacy

Hale collects nothing and never transmits your personal data. Full details:
[Privacy Policy](https://github.com/officialxndr/KUfit/blob/main/docs/PRIVACY.md).
